About Me

I am a PhD candidate at the ACT Lab, School of Computer Science and Engineering, Beihang University, supervised by Prof. Hailong Sun. My research investigates privacy-preserving computation, fairness evaluation for federated learning, and how large models can collaborate with distributed data. I am a key contributor to China's National Key R&D Program on industrial IoT data analytics, participate in defense and national science foundation projects, and co-author the MIIT textbook on crowdsourcing data annotation.
